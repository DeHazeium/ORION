ORION

Optimized Real-time Interface for Output Networks

An IoT energy-monitoring system that measures live mains power with a PZEM-004T sensor on an ESP32, streams readings to the cloud in real time, and calculates electricity cost against the Sarawak Energy domestic tariff — viewable from anywhere on this dashboard.
